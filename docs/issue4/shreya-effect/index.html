The Quest For The Sea's Blue: The Discovery of the Raman Effect

Shreya Ganguly
IISER Kolkata
Submitted: May 10, 2025

Dicover how a simple question - why does the sea look blue - led to one of the most groundbreaking discoveries in physics. This engaging science comic traces C.V.Raman’s journey to uncover the phenomenon that now bears his name.

Shreya Ganguly is a student of 22MS batch pursuing a physics major at IISER Kolkata. A habitual explorer and art-lover, she likes drawing, dancing and trying out her hand at graphic design and video editing - when she isn’t drowning in coursework, that is.
